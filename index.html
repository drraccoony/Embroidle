Embroidle

How to play Embroidle!

Rules

The rules of Embroidle are elegantly simple. Your objective is to guess a secret multi-letter word in as few guesses as possible. To submit a guess, type any word and press enter.

  • Each guess must be a valid word.
  • The wordbank is embroidery / sewing related.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Examples

S T I T C H

S is in the word and in the correct spot.

T H R E A D

D is in the word but in the wrong spot.

D E S T G N

T is not in the word in any spot.
